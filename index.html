About DataBuddy

DataBuddy is a lightweight, offline-first application designed to help creators, analysts, and developers manage and visualize their data securely. With a focus on simplicity and performance, DataBuddy offers powerful tools for data editing, analysis, and encryption without relying on cloud services.

Whether you're working with CSV files, Excel spreadsheets, or JSON data, DataBuddy provides an intuitive interface to clean, organize, and gain insights from your datasets. The built-in AI-powered visualization features allow you to generate charts and summaries effortlessly, making data analysis accessible to everyone.

Developed with privacy in mind, all processing is done locally on your machine, ensuring that your sensitive information remains secure. DataBuddy is optimized for performance on older hardware, making it a versatile choice for users across different systems.
